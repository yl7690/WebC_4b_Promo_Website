Eason Chan
About E

Eason Chan (born 27 July 1974) is a prominent and highly representative Chinese singer and actor.

Chan was ranked number "6" in the 2013 Forbes China Celebrity Top 100 List.

Over 20 years since his musical debut, he released more than 40 albums and won numerous renowned awards in Hong Kong, China, Taiwan, and Southeast Asia.

Chan has been the most-streamed artist in Hong Kong on the Spotify music streaming platform from 2016 to 2020.

Music Awards(include but not limited to):

  • In 2003, he won Best Mandarin Male Singer and Best Mandarin Album for Special Thanks To...
  • In 2006 Chan's Cantonese album U87 was named one of Time magazine's "Five Asian Albums Worth Buying".
  • In 2009, he won Best Mandarin Album for Don't Want to Let Go.
  • Chan won his second Best Mandarin Male Singer award in 2015, for the album Rice and Shine.
  • In 2018, Chan was named Best Mandarin Male Singer for the third time - the most of any singer (tie with Johny Yin) - for the album C'mon In~.
